Decision Makers

Change.org

← PREV

As the world's largest and most succssful social change platform, Change.org has seen over 50 million people take action to make change. However, until now that has largely been a one-way proposition. With Change.org for Decision Makers, we set out to design the world's first two-way petition platform letting decision makers respond to and interact directly with their constituents and others.

Research, UX Design, UI Design, Visual Design, Optimization
