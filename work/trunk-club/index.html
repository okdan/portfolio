Trunk Club is an innovative and customer-focused startup here in Chicago. Providing high quality and hand-selected clothing for men, shipped free to their door, user experience is paramount. I spent six months working as a product design consultant within Trunk Club focusing on three major projects: the iOS application, optimization of new customer acquisition, and trunkclub.com.

UX Design, UI Design, Visual Design, Optimization

In order to improve the onboarding for new users of trunkclub.com, we designed and a/b testing multiple variations and multiple concepts. The designs shown above are one example of the experience a new user would get after creating their account. In the end, we were able to significantly improve overall conversions, customer retention, and reduce the time between signing up and ordering your first trunk.
