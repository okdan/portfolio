About

For the past 8 years, I have been designing and building a wide range of digital projects for a wide range of clients and audiences. My core skills are in UI, UX, and visual design; but I’ve been writing code since the beginning of my career as well. Truly great digital work is the harmony of design and code. So while my focus is on design, it’s always been my goal to remain well versed in the ones and zeros of it all.

Interested in working together? Send me an email: dan@okdan.com
