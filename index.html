Dan Carson.

Head of Design and User Experience at Crowdpac

I’m an experienced design leader who believes design can make a positive difference in people’s lives. Over the past 14 years I’ve worked at agencies, on a Presidential campaign, consulted with non-profits and corporations, and built startups. I love to collaborate and work cross-functionally, using design to maximize potential, deliver value, and ultimately make an impact in the world.

Selected Work

Crowdpac is the leading political crowdfunding platform. We have helped hundreds of people run for office, from Library Board to City Council to President, and have helped them raise millions of dollars while connecting with their community of supporters.

As the Head of Design and the sole designer, I’ve spent the past two years building our design practice. I collaborate with engineers, product managers, and our founders to define our approach, understand our customers, and find product-market fit.
